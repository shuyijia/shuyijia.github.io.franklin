Shuyi Jia 「郏舒逸」

I am a third-year student at SUTD, majoring in Computer Science and Design (CSD), a CS degree sprinkled with doses of engineering, design and social sciences.

I am also a member of the pioneer batch of SHARP, a premier research-oriented programme that offers advanced classes and interdisciplinary research opportunities. I am currently working on interpretability of NLP models, mentored by Prof. Lu Wei. Previously, I have worked with Prof. Dario Poletti on applying machine learning methods to quantum many-body systems, and Prof. Cheong Kang Hao on Parrondo's paradox.

Research

My research interests lie in machine learning and computational science. With theoretical understanding, I'm also interested in doing research with a strong social impact and addressing fundamental problems in natural sciences, especially in the fields of computational physics and chemistry.
